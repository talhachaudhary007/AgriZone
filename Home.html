OUR MISSION

Our mission is to reduce poverty, raise incomes and improve food security for 80% of the world's poor, who live in rural areas and work mainly in farming. Since competition among local farmers is often tough, online agriculture markets can help boost marketing presence and helps save local farmers from closing their business. The aim is to boost agricultural and rural development by improving access to valuable information that can help agricultural stakeholders to make the best possible decisions and use the resources available in the most productive and sustainable manner.
