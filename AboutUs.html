OUR PUSPOSE

Purpose of this is intending to reduce the gap in the Agri value chain by connecting farmers with local market dealers (i.e., for modern tech & equipment’s) and end consumers (i.e., direct home customers), making sure that farmers get a fair price and our customers, top quality produce. Normally 80% of the farmers are those who actually not aware to advance technology and can't afford modern equipment’s, here we give platform where they took on rent easily. Initially we start it with online website and helpline for all order placements. We'll maintain a standard for delivery of fresh fruits and vegetables, straight from the farm, to your doorstep and secondly for the ease of farmer so they can sell their crops and also buy seed, fertilizers, pesticides and use modern technology equipment’s. Our focus is on catering to the comfort and ease of our customers and ensuring they enjoy crops, fresh fruits and vegetables. Our team is passionate and committed to our cause, that we can help farmers, and at the same time bring their farm-fresh goodness and crops to our customer's home.

BUILD AGRI CHAIN

Living in developing countries, where a large percentage of our labor force works in the agriculture sector, we believe it is our job to contribute to the community by making farming a profitable business. The system allows farmers to sell their stock directly as a direct supplier throughout the country without any middlemen so that, farmer earns optimum rates for his stock and also the customer gets it at lowered costs. We play a role in the Agri value chain by connecting farmers to end-users and giving them a fair price for their produce in return. We buy products directly from the farmers and deliver to our customers. We believe it is our job to inform and promote the importance of consuming healthy and fresh produce and make it easily accessible to our customers. Enabling our customers to consume fresh fruits and vegetables that too at reasonable rates, without even having to step out of their house. The comfort, convenience and health of our customers is our top priority and we promise to always provide them with fresh, top-quality products while at the same time making a difference in the agriculture community of Pakistan.
